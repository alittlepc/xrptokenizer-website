Protocol Specifications (FAQ)

How is 1:1 Parity maintained? +
Every IAW token is mathematically pegged to RLUSD. The Hook prevents any minting or movement unless backed 1:1 by reserves in the transparent IAW Vault.
What is the 0.33% Integrity Fee? +
A hard-coded 0.33% fee ($0x21) is applied at the ledger level to all institutional transactions. This ensures sub-second settlement and network maintenance.
Why no Liquidity Pools? +
We utilize Just-In-Time (JIT) settlement. This eliminates slippage and impermanent loss, ensuring institutions always exit at exactly 1:1 value.
How do Redemptions work? +
We use a "Vault and Release" mechanism. Tokens are returned to the IAW Vault, which triggers an automated release of RLUSD. No tokens are burned, preserving the audit trail.
